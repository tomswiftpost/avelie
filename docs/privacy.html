Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

What Avelie Stores

Avelie stores profile details, symptom logs, sleep entries, cycle entries, medication tracking, notes, article progress, and app settings that you choose to enter.

Health data is only imported when you enable Apple Health access. Avelie currently reads sleep analysis to help prefill sleep logs.

Where Data Is Stored

Tracking data is stored on your device using SwiftData. Avelie does not sync tracking data to an Avelie server in this build.

Apple device backups may include app data depending on your iCloud or device backup settings.

Avelie does not sell your personal tracking data.

Purchases

Avelie uses RevenueCat to process subscription status and purchase entitlements. RevenueCat may receive purchase, device, app user identifier, and entitlement information needed to manage subscriptions.

Apple processes App Store payments under Apple terms and privacy policies.

Notifications And Exports

If you enable reminders, Avelie schedules local notifications on your device. Notification settings can be changed in Avelie Settings or system Settings.

When you export or share reports, you choose where the exported files go. Those destinations are outside Avelie.

Deletion And Contact

You can delete local Avelie data from Settings. Deleting the app also removes local app data from the device. Apple Health permissions can be changed in the iOS Settings app.

For privacy questions, contact avenlio.support@gmail.com.
